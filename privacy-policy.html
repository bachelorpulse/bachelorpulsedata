Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Bachelor Pulse. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle information for our NCERT MCQ Practice platform.

For our ₹29 premium subscription, all transactions are processed through secure, third-party payment gateways (like Razorpay/Paytm). Bachelor Pulse does not store your credit/debit card or UPI details on our servers.

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website. You may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Our content is designed for students in Classes 6–10. We encourage parents and guardians to observe and guide their child's online activity. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

5. Data Security

Your data is precious. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your information.
