Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to BachelorPulse ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our website bachelorpulse.in and our Android application available on the Google Play Store (collectively, the "Platform"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information depending on how you use the Platform:

  • Google Account Data (if you sign in): When you log in using "Continue with Google", we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We use this only to create and identify your account.
  • Username: A unique username you choose during account setup, stored to identify your profile.
  • Quiz & Performance Data: Your quiz scores, chapters attempted, correct/wrong answers, streaks, and time spent — used to power your Performance Dashboard.
  • Bookmarks: Questions you save for later review, stored against your account.
  • Badges & Achievements: Learning milestones you earn, stored to your profile.
  • Device Information (App only): Device model, OS version, and app version — collected automatically to diagnose crashes and improve performance. No personally identifiable device data is stored.
  • Usage Data: Pages/screens visited and general interaction patterns, used to improve content and the Platform.

We do not collect: precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, SMS, or any sensitive device permissions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account on BachelorPulse.
  • To save and sync your quiz progress, bookmarks, badges, and streaks across sessions and devices.
  • To display your performance dashboard and personalised learning insights.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve Platform stability.
  • To improve content quality based on aggregate usage patterns.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Firebase & Google Services

BachelorPulse uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication, database, and crash reporting. Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore with strict Security Rules — you can only read and write your own data.

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles Google Sign-In. We never see or store your Google password.
  • Firebase Firestore: Stores your profile, quiz history, badges, and bookmarks. Security Rules ensure no other user can access your data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (App): Collects anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. No personal data is included in crash reports.
  • Firebase privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. App Permissions (Android App)

Our Android app requests only the minimum permissions needed:

  • Internet: Required to load content, sync progress, and serve ads.
  • Network State: To detect connectivity and show appropriate offline messages.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive permission.

6. Payment Information

For any premium subscription, all transactions are processed through secure third-party payment gateways (such as Razorpay/Paytm). BachelorPulse does not store your credit/debit card or UPI details on our servers.

7. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

We also use browser storage (localStorage) to save your quiz progress locally on your device when you are not signed in.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for students in Classes 6–10 (typically ages 11–16). We encourage parents and guardians to guide their child's online activity. For users under 13, we recommend parental consent before creating an account. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

The login system is entirely optional — all MCQ practice features are available without creating an account.

9. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data (username, quiz history, badges, bookmarks, profile). To do so:

  • Email us at sirshashwatsingh@gmail.com with subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • We will permanently delete your data from Firebase Firestore within 7 working days.
  • This action is irreversible. Your progress, badges, and bookmarks cannot be recovered after deletion.

10. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase Security Rules, HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, and regular security reviews. Access to your data is restricted strictly to your own account.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to external websites (e.g. YouTube). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as we add new features. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version. Continued use of BachelorPulse after changes means you accept the updated policy.
