Class 6 Science Ch 1: A Wonderful World of Science

A journey into curiosity, exploration, and the fundamental way we understand our universe.

✨ Chapter Summary

Science isn't just a subject; it's a way of looking at the world with curiosity and wonder. This chapter introduces students to the habit of asking 'Why' and 'How', leading to the path of discovery.

  • Curiosity as Fuel: Every scientific breakthrough starts with a simple question.
  • Observation: The art of looking carefully to find patterns in nature.
  • The Scientific Process: From forming a hypothesis to testing it through experiments.
  • A Collective Effort: How different branches like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology work together.
  • Constant Evolution: Scientific knowledge is never finished; it grows as we find new evidence.
